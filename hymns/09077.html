HYMN LXXVII. Indu.

MORE beauteous than the beautiful, as Indra's bolt, this Soma, rich in sweets, hath clamoured in the vat.

Dropping with oil, abundant, streams of offering flow unto him like milch-kine, lowing, with their milk.
