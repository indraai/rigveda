HYMN CVII. Devas.

THE sacrifice obtains the Devas’ acceptance: be graciously inclined to us, Ādityas.

Hitherward let your favour be directed, and be our best deliverer from trouble.

By praise-songs of Aṅgirases exalted. May the Devas come to us with their protection. May Indra with his powers, Devas with Devas, Aditi with Ādityas grant us shelter.

This laud of our. May Indra and Indra, Aryaman Agni, Indra find pleasant. This prayer of our. May Indra grant, and Mitra, and Aditi and Sindhu, Earth and Heaven.
