HYMN XLVIL Vāyu. Indra-Vāyu.

Vā

Vāyu, the bright is offered thee, best of the meath at holy rites.

Come thou juice, Deva, longed-for, on thy team-drawn car.

O Vāyu, thou and Indra are meet drinkers of these Soma-draughts, for unto you the drops proceed as waters gather to the vale.

O Indra-Vāyu, mighty Twain, speeding together, Lords of Strength, Come to our succour with your team, that y. May drink the Soma juice.

The longed-for teams which ye possess, O Heroes, for the worshipper, Turn to us, Indra-Vāyu, ye to whom the offering is paid.
