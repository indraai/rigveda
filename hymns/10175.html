HYMN CLXXV. Press-stones.

MAY Indra the Deva, O Stones, stir you according to the Law: Be harnessed to the shafts, and press.

Stones, drive calamity away, drive ye away malevolence: Make ye the Cows our medicine.

Of one accord the upper Stones, giving the Bull his bull-like strength, Look down with pride on those below.

May Indra the Deva, O Stones, stir you as Law commands for him Who offers, pouring juice.
