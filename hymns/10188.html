HYMN CLXXXVIII. Agni.

Now send ye Jā

Now send ye Jātavedas forth, send hitherward the vigorous Steed To seat him on our sacred grass.

I raise the lofty eulogy of Jātavedas, raining boons, with sages for his hero band.

With flames of Jātavedas which carry oblation to the Devas, May he promote our offering.
