HYMN CLXXXVIII. Agni.

Now send ye Jﾄ》avedas forth, send hitherward the vigorous Steed To seat him on our sacred grass.

I raise the lofty eulogy of Jﾄ》avedas, raining boons, with sages for his hero band.

With flames of Jﾄ》avedas which carry oblation to the Devas, may he promote our offering.

沒 沒 汨 汨
