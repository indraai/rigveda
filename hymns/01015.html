O Indra with Ṛtu.

Devas, with Ṛtu; sanctify the rite, for ye give precious gifts.

O Neṣṭar, with thy Dame accept our offering; with Ṛtu drink, for thou art he who giveth wealth.

Bring the Devas, in the three appointed places set them down: Surround them, and with Ṛtu drink.

Ṛtus, from the Brāhmaṇa's bounty: undissolved, O Indra, is thy friendship's bond.

Mitra, Varuṇa, ye whose ways are firm&, with Ṛtu ye have reached the rite.

Praise the Wealth-giver in the rite, In offerings praise the Deva.

May the Wealth-giver grant to us riches that shall be far renowned.

These things we gain, among the Devas.

He with the Ṛtu fain would drink, Wealth-giver, from the Neṣṭar's bowl. Haste, give your offering, and depart.

Wealth-giver, honour thee with the Ṛtus, be A Giver bountiful to us.

Drink ye the meath, O Aśvins bright with flames, whose acts are pure, who with Ṛtus accept the offering.

With Ṛtu, kind Giver, guidest offering: Worship the Devas for the pious man.
