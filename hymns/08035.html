HYMN XXXV. Indra.

WITH Agni and with Indra, Indra. Indra, Somas, Devas, closely leagued; Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Indra, drink the Soma juice.

With all the Holy Thoughts, all being Mighty Ones! in close alliance wil the Mountains. Heaven, and Earth; Accordant. of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Indra, drink the Soma juice.

With all the Deities, three times eleven, here, in close alliance with the Devas, Bhṛgus, Floods; Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Indra, drink the Soma juice.

Accept the offering, attend to this my call: come nigh, O ye Twain Devas, to all libations here.

Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Indra, bring us strengthening food.

Accept our praise-song as a youth accepts a maid. Come nigh, O ye Twain Devas, to all libations here. Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Indra, bring us strengthening food.

Accept the songs we sing, accept the solemn rite. Come nigh, O ye Twain Devas, to all libations here. Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Indra, bring us strengthening food.

Ye fly as starlings fly unto the forest trees; like buffaloes ye seek the Soma we have shed. Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, come thrice, O Indra, to our home.

Ye fly like swans, like those who travel on their way; like buffaloes ye seek the Soma we have shed. Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, come thrice, O Indra, to our home.

Ye fly to our oblation like a pair of hawks; like buffaloes ye seek the Soma we have shed. Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, come thrice, O Indra, to our home.

Come hitherward and drink and satisfy yourselves, bestow upon us progeny and affluence. Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Indra, grant us vigorous strength.

Conquer your foes, protect us, praise your worshippers; bestow upon us progeny and affluence. Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Indra, grant us vigorous strength.

Slay enemies, animate men whom ye befriend; bestow upon us progeny and affluence. Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Indra, grant us vigorous strength.

With Mitra, Indra, Dharma, and the Devas in your company approach unto your praiser's call. Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, and with the Ādityas, Indra! come.

With Indra and the Aṅgirases attending you, and with the Devas come unto your praiser's call. Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, and with the Ādityas, Indra! come.

With Ṛbhus and With Vājas. O ye Mighty Ones, leagued with the Devas come ye to your praiser's call. Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, and with the Ādityas, Indra! come.

Give spirit to our meditation and animate our thoughts; subdue ye the Rākṣasas and drive away disease. Accordant, of One mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, -the presser's Soma, Indra drink.

Strengthen the Ruling Power, strengthen the men of war; subdue ye the Rākṣasas and drive away disease. Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, the presser's Soma, Indra drink.

Give strength unto the milch-kine, give the people strength, subdue ye the Rākṣasas and drive away disease. Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, the presser's Soma, Indra drink.

As ye heard Atri's earliest eulogy, so hear Śyāvāśva ye who reel in joy. Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, drink juice, O Indra, three days old.

Further like running streams Śyāvāśva's eulogies who presses out the Soma, ye who reel in joy. Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, drink juice, O Indra, three days old.

Seize, as ye grasp the reins, Śyāvāśva's solemn rites who presses out the Soma, ye who reel in joy. Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, drink juice, O Indra, three days old.

Drive down your chariot hitherward drink ye the Soma's savoury juice. Approach, ye Indra, come to us: I call you, eager for your aid. Grant treasures to the worshipper.

When offering which tells our reverence hath begun. Heroes! to drink the gushing juice, Approach, ye Indra, come to us: I call you, eager for your aid. Grant treasures to the worshipper.

Sate you with consecrated drink, with juice effused, ye Deities. Approach, ye Indra, come to us: I call you, eager for your aid. Grant treasures to the worshipper.
