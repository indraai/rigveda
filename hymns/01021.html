HYMN XXI. Indra and Agni.

INDRA and Agni I invoke fain are we for their song of praise: Chief Soma-drinkers are they both.

Praise ye, O men, and glorify Indra and Agni in the holy rites: Sing praise to them in sacred songs.

Indra and Agni we invite.

Strong Devas, we bid them come to this libation that stands ready here: Indra and Agni, come to us.

Indra and Agni, mighty Lords of our assembly.

Watch ye, through this your truthfulness, there in the place of spacious view Indra and Agni, send us bliss.
