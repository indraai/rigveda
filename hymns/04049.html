RigVeda Hymn 04049

DEAR is this offering in your mouth, O Indra and Bṛhaspati: Famed is the laud, the gladdening draught.

This lovely Soma is effused, O Indra and Bṛhaspati, for you, to drink it and rejoice.

As Soma-drinkers to our house come, Indra and Bṛhaspati-and Indra-to drink Soma juice.

Vouchsafe us riches hundredfold, O Indra, and Bṛhaspati, with store of horses, thousandfold.

O Indra. and Bṛhaspati, we call you when the meath is shed, with songs, juice.

Drink, Indra and Bṛhaspati, the Soma in the giver's house: Delight yourselves abiding there.
