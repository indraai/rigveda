HYMN XLIX. Indra-Soma.

DEAR is this offering in your mouth, O Indra and Soma, Famed is the laud, the gladdening draught.

This lovely Soma is effused, O Indra and Soma, for you, to drink it and rejoice.

As Soma-drinkers to our house come, Indra and Soma-and Indra-to drink Soma juice.

Vouchsafe us riches hundredfold, O Indra, and Soma, with store of horses, thousandfold.

O Indra. and Soma, we call you when the meath is shed, with songs, juice.

Drink, Indra and Soma, the Soma in the giver's house: Delight yourselves abiding there.
