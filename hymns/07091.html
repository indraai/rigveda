Guardians infallible, eager as envoys' preserve us safe through many months and autumns.

Addressed to you, our fair praise, Indra-Soma, implores your favour and renewed well-being.

Wise, bright, arranger of his teams, he. seeketh men with rich food whose treasures are abundant. They have arranged them of one mind with Soma: the men have wrought all noble operations.

So far as native power and strength permit you, so far as men behold whose eyes have vision, O ye pure-drinkers, drink with us pure Soma: sit on this sacred grass, Indra and Soma.

Driving down teams that bear the lovely Heroes, hitherward, Indra-Soma, come together. To you this prime of savoury juice is offered: here loose your horses and be friendly-minded.

Your hundred and your thousand teams, O Indra and Soma, all-munificent, which attend you, with these most gracious-minded come ye hither, and drink, O Heroes of the meath we offer.

Like coursers seeking fame will we Vasisthas, O Indra-Soma, with our fair laudations, Exerting all our powe-, call you to aid us. Preserve us evermore, ye Devas, with blessings.
