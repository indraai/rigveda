RigVeda Hymn 07052

MAY we be free from every bond, Ādityas! a castle among Devas and men, ye Devas.

Winning. May we win Varuṇa and Mitra, and, being. May we be, O Earth and Heaven.

May Varuṇa and Mitra grant this blessing, our Guardians, shelter to our seed and offspring. Let us not suffer for another's trespass. nor do the thing that ye, O Devas, punish.

The ever-prompt Aṅgirases, imploring riches from Indra the Deva, obtained them. May our Father who is great and holy, and all the Devas, accordant, grant this favour.
