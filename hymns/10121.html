HYMN CXXI. Ka.

IN the beginning rose Hiranyagarbha, born Only Lord of all created beings.

He fixed and holdeth up this Earth and Heaven. What Deva shall we adore with our oblation?

Giver of vital breath, of power and vigour, he whose commandments all the Devas acknowledge -. The Lord of death, whose shade is life immortal. What Deva shall we adore with our oblation?

Who by his grandeur hath become Sole Ruler of all the moving world that breathes and slumbers; He who is Loord of men and Lord of cattle. What Deva shall we adore with our oblation?
