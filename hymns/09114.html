HYMN CXIV. Indu.

THE man who waIketh as the Laws of Indu Indu bid,— Men call him rich in children, him, O Soma, who hath met thy thought. Flow, indu, flow for Indra's sake.

Kasyapa, Ṛṣi, lifting up thy voice with hymn-composers' lauds, Pav reverence to King Soma born the Sovran Ruler of the plants. Flow, indu, flow for Indra's sake.

Seven regions have their several Suns; the ministering monks are seven; Seven are the Āditya Deities,—with these, O Soma, guard thou us. Flow, indu, flow for Indra's sake.

Guard us with this oblation which, King Soma, hath been dressed for thee.
