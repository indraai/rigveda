HYMN XCVIII. Agni.

STILL in Vaiśvānara's grac. May we continue: yea, he is King supreme o’er all things living.

Sprung hence to life upon this All he looketh. Vaiśvānara hath rivalry with Sūrya.

Present in Heaven, in Earth, all-present Agni,—all plants that grow on ground hath he pervaded. May Agni. May Vaiśvānara with vigour, present, preserve us day and night from foemen.

Be this thy truth to us-ward: let wealth in rich abundance gather round us. This prayer of our. May Indra grant, and Mitra, and Aditi and Sindhu, Earth and Heaven.
