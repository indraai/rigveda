HYMN X. Agni.

HE hath sent forth, bright, radiant, and refulgent his far-spreading lustre.

Pure in his splendour shines the golden Hero: our longing thoughts hath he aroused and wakened.

He, like the Sun, hath shone while Morn is breaking, and monks who weave the offering sing praises, Agni, who knows their generations and visits Devas, most bounteous, rapid envoy.

Our songs and holy hymns go forth to Agni, seeking the Deva, him fair to see, of heroic nature, mighty, men's messenger who carries their oblations.
