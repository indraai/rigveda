HYMN XXXVIII. Devas.

WHAT now? When will ye take us by both hands, as a dear sire his son, Devas, for whom sacred grass is clipped?

Now whither? To what goal of yours go ye in Heaven, and not on Earth? Where do your cows disport themselves?

Where are your newest favours shown? Where, Devas, your prosperity? Where all your high felicities?
