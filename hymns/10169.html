HYMN CLXIX. COWS.

MAY the wind blow upon our Cows with healing. May they eat herbage full of vigorous juices.

May they drink waters rich in life and fatness: to food that moves on feet be gracious, Soma.

Like-coloured, various-hued, or single-coloured, whose names through offering are known to Agni, whom the Aá¹…girases produced by Fervour.vouchsafe to these, Parjanya, great protection.
