HYMN XVIII. Agni.

AT dawn let Agni, much-beloved guest of the house, be glorified; Immortal who delights in all oblations brought by mortal men.

For Dvita who receives through wealth of native strength maimed offerings, Thy praiser even gains at once the Soma-drops, Immortal Devas!

Nobles, with song I call that car of yours that shines with lengthened life, for, Deva who givest steeds! that car hither and thither goes unharmed.

They who have varied ways of thought, who guard the lauds within their lips, and strew the grass before the light, have decked themselves with high renown.
