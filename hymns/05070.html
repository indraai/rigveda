HYMN LXX. Mitra-Varuṇa.

EVEN far and wide, O Varuṇ

EVEN far and wide, O Varuṇa and Mitra, doth your grace extend.

May I obtain your kind good-will.

From you, benignant Devas. May we gain fully food for sustenance. Such, O ye Somas, my we be.

Guard us, O Somas. with your guar4 save us, ye skilled to save, my we Subdue the Dasyus, we ourselves,

Or ne’e. May we, O Wondrous Strong, enjoy another's solemn feast, Ourselves, our sons, or progeny.
