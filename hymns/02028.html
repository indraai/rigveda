Loose me from sin as from a bond that binds me. May we swell, Indra, thy spring of Order. Let not my thread, while I weave song, be severed, nor my work's sum, before the time, be shattered.

Far from me, Indra, remove all danger accept me graciously, thou Holy Sovran. Cast off, like cords that hold a calf, my troubles: I am not even mine eyelid's lord without thee.
