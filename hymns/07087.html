Indra's spies, sent forth upon their errand, survey the two world-halves well formed and fashioned. Wise are they, holy, skilled in offers, the furtherers of the praise-songs of the prudent.

Like Indra from Heaven he sinks in Sindhu, like a white-shining spark, a strong wild creature. Ruling in depths and meting out the region, great saving power hath he, this world's Controller.
