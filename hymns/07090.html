HYMN XC. Soma.

To you pure juice, rich in meath, are offered by priest: through longing for the Pair of Heroes.

Drive, Soma, bring thine harnessed horses hither: drink the pressed Soma till it make thee joyful.

Whoso to thee, the Mighty, brings oblation, pure Soma unto thee, pure-drinking Soma, that man thou makest famous among mortals: to him strong sons are born in quick succession.
