HYMN XXXIX. Indra.

STONE-DARTING Indra. Wondrous One, what wealth is richly given from thee, that bounty, Treasure-Finder! bring filling both thy hands, to us.

Bring what thou deemest worth the wish, O Indra, that which is in Heaven.

May we know thee as thou art, boundless in thy munificence.
