HYMN LI. Devas.

WITH all assistants, Agni, come hither -juice; With Devas unto our sacred gifts.

Come to the offering, O ye whose ways are right, whose laws are true, and drink the draught with Agni's tongue.

O Singer, with the singers, O Gracious, with those who move at dawn, Come to the Soma-draught with Devas.

To Indra and to Soma dear, this Soma, by the mortar pressed, is now poured forth to fill the jar.

Soma, come hither to the feast, wellpleased unto our sacred gifts: Drink of the Soma juice effused come to the food.

Ye, Indra, Soma, well deserve to drink the juices pressed by us.

Gladly accept them, spotless Pair come to the food.

For Indra and for Soma pressed are Soma juices blent with curd, as rivers to the lowland flow: come to the food.

Associate with all the Devas, come, with the Indra and with Dawn, Agni, as erst with Atri, so enjoy the juice.
