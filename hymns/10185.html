HYMN CLXXXV. Aditi.

GREAT, unassailable must he the heavenly favour of Three Devas, Varuṇa, Mitra, Aryaman.

O'er these, neither at home nor yet abroad or pathways that are Strange, the evil-minded foe hath power

Nor over him. the man on whom the Sons of Aditi bestow Eternal light that h. May live.
