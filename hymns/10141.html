HYMN CXLI. Viśvedevas.

TURN hither, Agni, speak to us: come to us with a gracious mind.

Enrich us, Master of the house: thou art the Giver of our wealth.

Let Aryarnan vouchsafe us wealth, and Bhaga, and Bṛhaspati. Let the Devas give their gifts, and let Sūnṛtā, Goddess, grant us wealth.

We call King Soma to our aid, and Agni with our songs and hymns, Ādityas, Viṣṇu, Sūrya, and the Brahman Monk Bṛhaspati.

Indra, Vāyu, Devas swift to listen, we invoke, that in the synod all the fol. May be benevolent to us.

Urge Aryaman to send us gifts, and Indra, and Bṛhaspati, Vāta, Viṣṇu, Sarasvatī and the Strong Courser Indra.

Do thou, O Agni, with thy fires strengthen our meditation and offering: Urge givers to bestow their wealth to aid our service of the Devas.
