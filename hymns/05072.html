RigVeda Hymn 05072

To Varuṇa and Mitra we offer with songs, as Atri did. Sit on the sacred grass juice.

By Ordinance and Law ye dwell in peace secure, bestirring men.

Sit on the sacred grass juice.

May Varuṇa and Mitra, for our help, accept the offering. Sit on the sacred grass juice.
