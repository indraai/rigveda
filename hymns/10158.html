HYMN CLVIII. Sūrya.

MAY Sūrya guard us out of Heaven, and Vāta from the Firmament, and Agni from terrestrial spots.

Thou Indra whose flame deserves hundred libations, be thou pleased: From failing lightning keep us safe.

May Indra the Deva, an. May Parvata also give us sight; May the Creator give us sight.

Give sight unto our eye, give thou our bodies sight that the. May see: May we survey, discern this world.

Thus, Sūrya. May we look on thee, on thee most lovely to behold, See clearly with the eyes of men.
