HYMN XL. Indra

DRINK, Indra;

DRINK, Indra; juice is shed to make thee joyful: loose thy Bay Steeds and give thy friends their freedom.

Begin the song, seated in our assembly. Give strength for offering to him who singeth.

Drink thou of this whereof at birth, O Indra, thou drankest, Mighty One for power and rapture. The men, the pressing-stones, the cows, the waters have made this Soma ready for thy drinking.

The fire is kindled, Soma pressed, O Indra: let thy Bays, best to draw, convey thee hither. With mind devoted, Indra, I invoke thee. Come, for our great prosperity approach us.

Indra, come hither: evermore thou camest through our great strong desire . Listen and hear the prayers which now we offer, and let this offering increase thy vigour.

Mayst thou, O Indra, on the day of trial, present or absent, wheresoe’er thou dwellest, thence, with thy team, accordant with the Devas, Song-lover! guard our offering, to help us.
