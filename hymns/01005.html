RigVeda Hymn 01005

O COME ye hither, sit ye down: to Indra sing ye forth, your song, companions, bringing hymns of praise.

To him the richest of the rich, the Lord of treasures excellent, Indra, with Soma.

May he stand by us in our need and in abundance for our wealth: May he come nigh us with his strength.

Whose pair of tawny horses yoked in battles foemen challenge not: To him, to Indra sing your song.

Nigh to Soma come, for his enjoyment, these pure drops.

Thou, grown at once to perfect strength, Strong Indra, for preëminence.

O Indra, lover of the song. May they bring bliss to thee the Sage.

Our chants of praise have strengthened thee, O Śatakratu. So strengthen thee the songs we sing.

Indra, accept these offerings thousandfold.

O Indra, thou who lovest song.
