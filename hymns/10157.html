HYMN CLVII. Viśvedevas.

WE will, with Indra and all Devas to aid us, bring these existing worlds into subjection.

WE will, with Indra and all Devas to aid us, bring these existing worlds into subjection.

Our offering, our bodies, and our offspr. let Indra form together with Ādityas.

With the Ādityas, with the band of Devas. May Indra be Protector of our bodies.

As when the Devas came, after they had slaughtered the Devass, keeping safe their Godlike nature,

Brought the Sun hitherward with mighty powers, and looked about them on their vigorous Godhead.
