HYMN LVIII. Indu.

SWIFT runs this giver of delight, even the stream of flowing juice: Swift runs this giver of delight.

The Morning knows all precious thingsdess knows her grace to man: Swift runs this giver of delight.

We have accepted thousands from Dhvasra's and Puruṣanti's hands: Swift runs this giver of delight.

From whom we have accepted thus thousands and three times ten beside: Swift runs this giver of delight.
