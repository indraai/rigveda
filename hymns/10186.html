HYMN CLXXXVI. Vāyu.

FILLING our hearts with health and joy. May Vāta breathe his balm on us May he prolong our days of life.

Thou art our Father, Vāta, yea, thou art a Brother and a friend, So give us strength that we may live.

The store of Amṛta laid away yonder, O Vāta, in thine home,— Give us thereof that we may live.
