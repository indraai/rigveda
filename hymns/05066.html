HYMN LXVI. Mitra-Indra.

O SAPIENT man, call the Two Devas, the very wise, who subdue the foe.

For Indra, whose form is Law, place offerings for his great delight.

For they have won unbroken sway in full perfection, power divine. And, like high laws, the world of man hath been made beautiful as light.

Therefore we praise you that your car. May travel far in front of ours- You who accept the eulogy of Ratahavya with his hymns.
