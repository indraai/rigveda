HYMN LX. Indu.

SING forth and laud with sacred song most active Indu, laud Indu who sees with thousand eyes.

Thee who hast thousand eyes to see, bearer of thousand burthens, they Have filtered through the fleecy cloth.

He, indu, hath streamed through the fleece then he runs into the jars, Finding his way to Indra's heart.

That Indr. May be bounteous, flow, most active Soma, for our weal: Bring genial seed with progeny.
