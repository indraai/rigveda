Indra and Agni, we invoke you both, as such, to send your cars: Lords of quick-coming bounty, ye who know, chief lovers of the song.

These who give increase day by day, Devas without guile for mortal man, Worthy themselves, I honour most, Two Devas as partners, for my horse.

The strength-bestowing offering thus to Indra and Agni hath been paid, as butter, purified by stones.

Deal to our princes high renown, deal wealth to those who sing your praise, deal food to those who sing your praise.
