HYMN CL. Agni.

THOU, bearer of oblations, though kindled, art kindled for the Devas.

THOU, bearer of oblations, though kindled, art kindled for the Devas.

With the Ādityas, Somas, Devas, come to us: to show us favour come to us.

Come hither and accept with joy this offering and hymn of ours. O kindled Deva, we mortals are invoking thee, calling on thee to show us grace.

I laud thee Jātavedas, thee Lord of all blessings, with my song. Agni, bring hitherward the Devas whose Laws we love, whose laws we love, to show us grace.

Agni the Deva was made the great High-Monk of Devas, Ṛṣis have kindled Agni, men of mortal mould. Agni I invocate for winning ample wealth. kindly disposed for winning wealth.

Atri and Bharadvāja and Gavisthira, Kaṇva and Trasadasyu, in our fight he helped. On Agni calls Vasiṣṭha, even the household priest, the household priest to win his grace.
