HYMN LIV. Indra.

URGE us not, Indra, for non. May comprehend the limit of thy strength.

Thou with fierce shout hast made the woods and rivers roar.

Sing hymns of praise to Indra, Lord of power and might; laud thou and magnify Indra who heareth thee, who with his daring might, a Bull exceeding strong in strength, maketh him master of the Heaven and Earth.

Sing forth to lofty Devas a strength-bestowing song, the Bold, whose resolute mind hath independent sway. High glory hath the Devas, compact of strength, drawn on by two Bay Steeds: a Bull, a Car is he.
