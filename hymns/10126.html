HYMN CXXVI. Devas.

No peril, no severe distress, ye Devas, affects the mortal man, whom Aryaman and Mitra lead, and Varima, of one accord, beyond his foes.

This very thing do we desire, Indra, Mitra, Aryaman, Whereby ye guhrd the mortal man from sore distress, and lead him safe beyond his foes.

These are, each one, our present helps, Indra, Mitra, Aryaman.

Best leaders, best deliverers to lead us on and bear as safe beyond our foes.

Ye compass round and guard each man, Indra, Mitra, Aryaman: In your dear keepin. May we be, ye who are excellent as guides beyond our foes.
