RigVeda Hymn 10131

DRIVE all our enemies away, O Indra, the western, mighty Conqueror, and the eastern.

Hero, drive off our northern foes and southern, that we in thy wide shelte. May be joyful.

What then? As men whose fields are full of barley reap the ripe corn removing it in order, So bring the food of those men, bring it hither, who went not to prepare the grass for worship.

Men come not with one horse at sacred seasons; thus they obtain no honour in assemblies. Sages desiring herds of kine and horses strengthen the mighty Indra for his friendship.

Ye, Aśvins, Lords of Splendour, drank full draughts of grateful Soma juice, and aided Indra in his work with Namuci of Devas birth.

As parents aid a son, both Aśvins, Indra, aided thee with their wondrous Powers and wisdom. When thou, with might. hadst drunk the draught that gladdens, Sarasvatī, O Indra, refreshed thee.

Indra is strong to save, rich in assistanc. May he, possessing all, be kind and gracious. May he disperse our foes and give us safety, an. May we be the lords of hero vigou r.

May we enjoy his favour, his the Hol. May we enjoy his blessed loving kindness. May this rich Indra, as our good Protector, drive off and keep afar all those who hate us.
