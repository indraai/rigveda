HYMN XXXIV. Indu.

THE drop of Soma juice effused flows onward with this stream impelled.

Rending strong places with its might.

Poured forth to Indra, Indra, to Soma and the Marut hosts, to Indra, flows the Soma juice.

With stones they press the Soma forth, the Strong conducted by the strong: They milk the liquor out with skill.
