HYMN CII Parjanya.

SING forth and laud Parjanya, son of Heaven, who sends the gift of rain May he provide our pasturage.

Parjanya is the Deva who forms in kine, in mares, in plants of Earth, and womankind, the germ of life.

Offer and pour into his mouth oblation rich in savoury juice: May he forever give us food.
