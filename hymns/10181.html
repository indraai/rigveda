HYMN CLXXXI. Devas.

VASIṢṬHA mastered the Rathantara, took it from radiant Dhātar, Indra, and Indra, Oblation, portion of fourfold oblation, known by the names of Saprathas and Prathas.

These sages found what lay remote and hidden, the offering's loftiest secret essence.

From radiant Dhātar, Indra, and Indra, from Agni, Bharadvāja brought the Brhat.

They found with mental eyes the earliest Yajus, a pathway to the Devas, that had descended. From radiant Dhātar, Indra, and Indra, from Sūrya did these sages bring the Gharma.
