HYMN XI. Indra.

ALL sacred songs have magnified Indra expansive as the sea, the best of warriors borne on cars, the Lord, the very Lord of strength.

Strong in thy friendship, Indra, Lord of power and might, we have no fear.

We glorify with praises thee.

The gifts of Indra from of old, when to the praise-singers he gives the boon of substance rich in kine.

Sustainer of each sacred rite, Indra, the Thunderer, much-extolled.

Lord of the thunder.

I, Hero, through thy bounties am come to the flood addressing thee. Song-lover, here the singers stand and testify to thee thereof.
