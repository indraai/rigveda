HYMN II. Soma.

BEAUTIFUL Soma, come, hear our call.

Knowing the days, the singers glorify thee, Soma, with their hymns of praise.

Soma, thy penetrating stream goes forth unto the worshipper.

These, Indra-Soma, have been shed; come for our offered daintiesâ€™ sake: The drops are yearning for you both.

Well do ye mark libations, ye Soma and Indra, rich in spoil! So come ye swiftly hitherward.

Soma and Indra, come to what we hath prepared: Soon, Heroes, thus I make my meditation.

We call, and foe-destroying Indra, who make the oil-fed rite complete.

Mitra and Indra, through Law, lovers and cherishers of Law.

Our Sages, Mitra-Indra, wide dominion, strong by birth, vouchsafe us strength that worketh well.
