HYMN XIV. Devas.

Soma, Agni, come, to our service and our songs.

With all these Devas.

We have invoked thee; they, O Singer, sing thee songs of praise Agni, come hither with the Devas;

Invoke Indu, Indra, Agni, Soma, and all the Devas.

For you these offerings are poured forth that gladden and exhilarate, the meath-drops resting in the cup.

Having strewn the grass, with offerings and all things prepared.

Let the swift steeds who carry thee, thought-yoked and dropping holy oil. Bring the DevasH.

Adored, the strengtheners of Law, unite them, Agni, O bright of tongue.

Let them, O Agni, who deserve worship and prase solemn offering.

Away, from the Sun's realm of light, the wise invoking Monk shall bring All Devas awaking with the dawn.

With all the Devas, with Indra, Agni, Soma.

Hallow thou this our offering.

Harness the Red Mares to thy car, the Bays, the flaming ones: With those bring hitherward the Devas.
