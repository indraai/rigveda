HYMN CLXXII. Devas.

WONDERFUL let your coming be, wondrous with help, ye Bounteous Ones, Devas, who gleam as diamonds gleam.

Far be from us, O Devas, ye free givers, your impetuous shaft; Far from us be the stone ye hurl.

O Bounteous Givers, touch ye not, O Devas lift us up that we may live.
